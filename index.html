Blackjack

THE GAME

    Aim

    Finish the game with a higher total than the dealer, without exceeding 21. You automatically lose if you go beyond 21.


    How to Play

  1. We use a 52-card deck.
    • Number cards (2-10) score the value indicated on them.
    • The face cards (Jack, Queen, King) score 10 points.
    • Ace can either be 1 or 11, depending on what helps you the most.

  2. The dealer will have one card facing down.

  3. Draw as many cards as you want by clicking "Hit" as long as you haven't exceeded 21.

  4. Click "Stay" if you don't wish to draw more cards.

  5. The dealer will now reveal the faced-down card letting you know based on your totals if you've won or not.

  6. Good luck!
